Heathrow T5 IT problems

"Apparently the computer software told the baggage people that the flight had taken off. So everyone in the plane just watched as all their suitcases were taken back into the terminal instead of being loaded on." A passenger during a BBC interview. After long time spending on planning and constructing the Terminal 5 of the London Heathrow Airport, no one had doubts about the big future of the T5 and its success. But sometimes, little problems may cause a lot of financial damage.
