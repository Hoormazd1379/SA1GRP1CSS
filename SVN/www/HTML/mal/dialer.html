A Dialer is an electronic device that is connected to a telephone line to monitor the dialed numbers and alter them to provide services that otherwise require lengthy National or International access codes to be dialed.
It modifies the numbers depending on the time of day or country, allowing the user to subscribe to the service providers who offer the best rates.
Automated dialers can store names and telephone numbers and enable the user to easily contact people without having to remember phone numbers, or to be tracked by Caller ID.
Some automated dialers will also dial a telephone number at regular intervals until a person answers the phone sounding an alarm to let the caller know that the called party is on the line.
