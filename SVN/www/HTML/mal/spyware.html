Is one of the oldest and most widespread threats on the internet and is called spyware. It is a type of malware that infects your computer and gathers informations about you, which can be the websites you visit, the thing you download and even usernames, passwords, payment informations email you send and receive and much more.
A spyware is sneaky and attaches itself to your operating system. As often occur you might even permit a spyware to install itself when you agree the terms and conditions of what looks like a legitimate program on your computer.


" A spyware runs quietly in the background, collecting informations and monitoring your activities. "

In Germany and Switzerland, a spyware used or made by the government is called govware. A Govware is typically a trojan horse used to intercept communications from a suspect computer. In the USA, the term policeware has been used for similar purposes.
The use of the term spyware has eventually declined as the practice of tracking users has been pushed even further; major websites and data mining companies track the user not by fraudolent practices but by the default settings created for users and the terms-of-service agreements. A good example would be the practice of Facebook and other famous websites of tracking users'browser activities, linked to their identity even outside Facebook website itself.

CoolWebSearch (CWS)

CoolWebSearch, also known as CWS, is a spyware that installs itself on Microsoft Windows based computers. First appeared in 2003 it had numerous capabilities when successfully installed on a computer. Among which there is the possibility to change the infected computer web browser settings and create pop-ups ads whic redirect to other websites, including pornography sites, collect private infomration about the user and slow the connection speed down.
