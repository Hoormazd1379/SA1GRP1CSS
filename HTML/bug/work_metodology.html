Work Metodology

Why is it important?

It may seem obvious, but while programming software the right way of working applied can help prevent most bugs. There are several aspects to consider in the context of programming to avoid bugs.

Double check and testing

As when writing an essay, it is important to check the spelling and syntax of the code, thus avoiding most bugs, many of which are syntax errors. In addition, you should test the program several times as long as you have the resources to do so on multiple machines. By doing so, you can detect a substantial number of bugs.

Comunication is key

In the vast majority of cases you program an application in a team of programmers, using the versioning system (in our case SVN). It often happens that a team member introduces a bug or creates a conflict in files modified by others. It is important to tell the rest of the team what you have changed.

Comments

While programming, it is important to comment on your code, indicating the reasons and functions of the various parts of the code. By doing so, you can possibly reduce the time it takes to find bugs. In addition, other team members can understand the code and decrease the possibility of creating more bugs. By commenting on your code, you create information describing the software that can be used in the future to immediately understand the function of the program itself, reducing the time spent debugging (if necessary).
